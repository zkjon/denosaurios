## a journey millions of years into the past

for more than **160 million years**, dinosaurs dominated the earth.

they walked across continents that no longer exist, lived in ecosystems completely different from today, and disappeared long before humans appeared.

**denosaurios** is a small project dedicated to exploring that story:  
the history of the earth, the mesozoic era, and the creatures that inhabited our planet long before us.

## about this project

this site was created from a personal fascination with dinosaurs and the incredible history of the earth.

its goal is to explain, in a clear and accessible way, what our planet was like in the past and the creatures that inhabited it long before us.
