## the beginning of everything

about **4.540 million years ago**, the earth was nothing more than a mass of molten rock in formation.

there were no oceans  
there was no breathable atmosphere  
there was no life

only chaos, collisions and extreme heat.

everything started from a cloud of gas and dust:

- called **solar nebula**
- that collapsed by gravity
- forming the sun in the center

around it, the rest of the material began to group together.

the earth formed from:

- constant collisions of rocks
- accumulation of material
- violent impacts

for millions of years.

it was a completely hostile place:

- extreme temperatures
- constant volcanoes
- meteorite bombardment

one of the most important events:

> an object the size of mars impacted the earth

from that collision:

- debris arose in orbit
- that would end up forming the **moon**

---

## a world in transformation

little by little:

- the surface began to cool
- the crust formed
- the first oceans appeared

and with them… the possibility of life.
