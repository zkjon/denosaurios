## an invisible change

for millions of years, life on earth was microscopic.

but some organisms developed something new.

a process capable of changing the planet forever.

---

## photosynthesis

some bacteria began to use sunlight to obtain energy.

as a result, they released oxygen.

at first, this oxygen did not remain in the atmosphere.

it reacted with other elements.

---

over time, the amount of oxygen increased.

little by little, it began to accumulate in the atmosphere.

this caused one of the greatest changes in the planet's history.

---

## a crisis for life

oxygen was toxic to many of the existing organisms.

many disappeared.

but others adapted.

and that adaptation opened the door to more complex forms of life.

---
